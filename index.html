About Me

Hello! I'm Shrey, a graduate student at the International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad. I work in the Agents and Applied Robotics Group - towards the autonomy and payload transport applications of multi robot systems. My main interests lie in optimization, sensor fusion, machine learning, and their application in the domains of robot control, perception and planning. As a roboticist, I have a special proclivity towards optimization probablistic models. I am currently using model predictive control ith dual gradient descent optimization. When I'm not in front of a computer, or with my hands deep down some robot's internals, I'm probably playing football.
